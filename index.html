Advocacy

As a political science major, I always had the interest in working in politics or in the nonprofit industry and making a difference in communities. I used to work at Comite Civico del Valle, a non-profit organization located in Imperial County. Imperial County is the poorest County in the State of California and has the second highest unemployment rate in the United States so I gained a mass amount of experience working with underserved and disadvantaged communities. I attended and planned many events during my time there. I performed outreach to the hard to reach communities to listen to their needs and attended meetings with government officials to update them on our needs, the most notable government official being the Secretary of the Department of Energy of the United States. We spoke to the Secretary about the importance of lithium in our transition to green energy and reducing our reliance on fossile fuels. Also, the importance of building up Imperial County and making it an economic powerhouse due to the lithium found in the County. The experience I acquired in my tenure is unmatched and I continue to acquire more experience in advocacy by participating in local meetings as a volunteer.
